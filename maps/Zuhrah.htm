Directions to Zuhrah Shrine Center
2540 Park Ave.
Minneapolis, MN
  • Phone: 612.871.3555
  • Zuhrah Shrine Center’s Fenced Parking Lot Entrance on Oakland between 25th and 26th Street
  • From 94 West: Take 94 West to 11th Street exit. Take a quick right on Grant Street and go to Portland. Turn right on Portland to 25th Street. Turn left on 25th and go one block. Turn right on Oakland and park in our lot or on the street.
  • From 94 East: Take 94 East to Hiawatha. Take the Cedar Avenue exit. Turn right onto Cedar Avenue to 26th Street. Turn right onto 26th Street to Oakland Avenue. Turn right onto Oakland Avenue and go one-half block. Park in our lot on your left or on the street.
  • From 35W North: Take 35W North to the 31st/Lake Street exit. Turn right onto either 31st Street or onto Lake Street. Turn left onto Oakland Avenue. Park in our lot on your left or on the street.
  • From 35W South: Take 35W South to the Hiawatha exit. From Hiawatha, take the Cedar Avenue exit. Turn right onto 26th Street to Oakland Avenue. Turn right onto Oakland Avenue and go one-half block. Park in our lot or on the street.
