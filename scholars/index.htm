Mission Statement
To Enable and Encourage Children in the Philippines to Stay in School by
providing Financial Scholarships and Assistance based upon Need.
For more information visit: Philippine Scholars Site
To be a Sponsor:
  • Send one (tax deductible) donation each quarter
  • Receive letters or email from the Scholar and a Report Card Summary
  • Send at least one letter or email per year to acknowledge the Scholar's effort
To be a Scholar:
  • Attend bimonthly meetings
  • Write to the Sponsor at least two letters or emails each quarter
    (each month if college)
  • Provide a copy of report card at year's end
