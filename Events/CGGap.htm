Cultural/Generation Gap Seminar
 

Bridging The Gap” was a series of workshops designed to foster an open dialogue between the Filipino youth and adults, to deal with cultural and generational issues within the community. Some of the discussion topics included:

• Growing up in a bi-racial family

• Cultural differences between the American born Filipino-Americans and recent Filipino immigrants

• Maintaining your cultural identity

• Braking down the Filipino sterotypes

• Living with Tradition Filipino parents

• Differences between American and Filipino culture
   
We'd like to thank all those who participated in the workshop to help make it a success.
