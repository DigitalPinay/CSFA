Children's Camp - Camp Sacajawea, Eagan
(Sat. - Sun., Jun. 16-17)     
 

This annual CSFA project is open to kids from 6 to 12 years of age and to those interested in participating in activities that will make being Flipino more fun and the Philippine a bit more interesting. In this camp, kids have been taught songs, told folk stories, danced traditional Filipino dances, and learned a few words and phrases. Contact Ligaya at st.ligaya@gmail.com for any questions.
