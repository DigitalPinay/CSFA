The CSFA is a non-profit organization dedicated since its inception in 1972 to the promotion and preservation of Filipino culture. Among the regular projects of the CSFA are:

PAMANA an entertaining musical and dance production showcasing the best of Filipino folk dances.
Dance Troupe weekly folk dance lessons, regular performances in community events and local happening, representation at the Festival of nations State Fair, Winter Carnival, Aquatennial, Asian festivals, and similar activities.
Philanthropy proceeds from caroling and other projects donated to the Philippine natural Disaster Relief Fund and local causes
Philippine Day an annual gathering in celebration of the Filipino culture.
Community Service
and Involvement		 information to and about the community through newsletters, phone directory, and other publications; outreach activities; participation and leadership involvement in other community organizations
