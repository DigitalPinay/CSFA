The CSFA Dance Troupe invites dancers of all ages
to have fun learning Filipino Folk Dances.
Our family oriented dance lessions/practices are conducted
Thursday nights, 7:00-9:00 pm at 977 5th St. E., St. Paul, MN 55106 *

Performances are performed throughout the year.
Events include banquets, wedding receptions, Diversity programs,
MN State Fair, Festival of Nations and many others. If interested, feel
free to stop by our practices to observe.

To request a dance performance or participate in dancing: 
Send an email to dancetroupe@csfamn.org
Feel free to bring a casette tape to record music
to practice at home.

* times and location are subject to change
